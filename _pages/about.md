I am a PhD student in the [Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering](https://engineering.wisc.edu/departments/chemical-biological-engineering/) at the **University of Wisconsin-Madison**, working with [Prof. Styliani Avraamidou](https://avraamidou.che.wisc.edu/) in the [Circular Economy Systems Engineering Group](https://avraamidou.che.wisc.edu/). I am also pursuing an M.S. in Industrial and Systems Engineering (Operations Research, Optimization, and Analytics).

My research sits at the intersection of **mathematical optimization**, **control theory**, and **machine learning**, applied to complex chemical and energy systems. I am broadly interested in developing computationally efficient frameworks for decision-making under uncertainty.

Previously, I completed my M.Tech. in Chemical Engineering from **IIT Bombay** (CGPA: 9.76/10, 2021–2023), where I worked with Prof. Ravindra Gudi and Prof. Abhilasha Maheshwari on digital twins and graph neural network-based fault detection and diagnosis. I hold a B.Eng. from **MSU Baroda** (GPA: 3.98/4.00, Gold Medal, 2016–2020).

## Research Interests

- **Multi-parametric Programming** — explicit solutions to parametric optimization problems; applications to MPC and Benders decomposition
- **Distributed MPC** — reducing computation and communication overhead in networked control systems using facet properties and mp-programming
- **Stochastic Optimization** — two-stage and multi-stage models for planning under uncertainty (Power-to-X, electricity markets)
- **Graph Neural Networks** — graph auto-encoders and attention-based GCNs for fault detection and diagnosis in process systems
- **Energy Systems** — water-energy-carbon nexus, data center infrastructure, hybrid vehicle control
