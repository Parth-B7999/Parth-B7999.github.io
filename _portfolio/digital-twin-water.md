title: "Digital Twin for Large-Scale Water Distribution Networks"

collection: portfolio
---

Developed an EPANET software-based digital twin of large-scale water distribution networks using differential evolution (DE) optimization for calibration.

- Applied Graph Convolution Networks (GCN) for pipe leak localization
- Water quality regulation on the benchmark C-Town network
- Framework for real-time operational decision support

**Paper:** [Digital Chemical Engineering, 2023](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.dche.2023.100127)
