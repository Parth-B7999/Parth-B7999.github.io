Developed a versatile generator for bilevel optimization problems, supporting various combinations of LP/QP and MILP/MIQP at both the upper and lower levels.

- Validates problem non-triviality using high-point relaxation methods
- Solves for global solutions with multi-parametric programming
- Used in the preprint: *Parametric Region Search: A Mixed-Integer Bilevel Optimization Problem Primal Heuristic* (Tsai, Brahmbhatt, Avraamidou, 2026)
